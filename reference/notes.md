## Reference notes (2–3 lines each)

- Greenhill et al. 2008 (ABVD): Primary Austronesian wordlist/cognate resource; our corpus source.
- List et al. 2022 (Lexibank): Standardized wordlists w/ phonetic transcriptions; underpins ABVD Lexibank format.
- Glottolog 4.x: Classification metadata; future RF/tree comparisons.
- Levenshtein 1966: Base edit-distance metric.
- List & Moran 2013 (SCA): Sound-class Levenshtein toolkit; justifies sound-class variant.
- Kondrak 2002 (PhD): Algorithms for language reconstruction; weighted/string methods.
- Saitou & Nei 1987 (NJ): Core phylogenetic tree algorithm used.
- Gascuel 1997 (BIONJ): Improved NJ; relevant alternative.
- Robinson & Foulds 1981: Tree distance for structural comparisons.
- Felsenstein 2004: Classic phylogenetics reference (book).
- Blust 2013 (Austronesian Languages): Authoritative background on Austronesian subgrouping.
- Gray & Atkinson 2003 (Nature): Dating and phylogenies; methodological exemplar.
- Gray, Drummond & Greenhill 2009 (PNAS): Pacific expansion pulses; Austronesian application.
- Bouckaert et al. 2012 (Science): Bayesian phylogeny; comparative case.
- Ringe, Warnow & Taylor 2002 (TAPA): Computational cladistics in linguistics.
- Kuleshov et al. 2018 (ICML): Calibration for regression; optional if discussing uncertainty.
- Gruber & Buettner 2022 (NeurIPS): Proper scoring rules; optional for calibration discussion.

## 新增引用（CL 投稿必要）

- Swadesh 1952 (lexico-statistic dating): 詞彙統計學/lexicostatistics 的歷史起點；任何 basic vocabulary 方法必引。
- Swadesh 1955 (improved lexicostatistics): 完善日期方法；CL reviewer 預期看到。
- Dolgopolsky 1986 (sound classes): Sound-class 方法的理論根源；使用 C/V sound class 必引。
- Wichmann et al. 2010 (ASJP evaluation): ASJP 的距離評估；作為自動分類方法的比較基準。
- Holman et al. 2008 (automated classification): ASJP 自動語言分類；與本研究 NJ 分類直接比較。
- Serva & Petroni 2008 (Levenshtein for IE): 用 normalized edit-distance 做語言距離的開創性工作。
- Jäger 2015 (PNAS): 從 ASJP 字串距離到系統發生推斷；與本研究方法鏈路直接可比。
- List et al. 2017 (automatic word comparison): LingPy 框架應用；automatic cognate detection。
- List 2012 (LexStat): SCA/LexStat 自動同源判定；CL 最常被要求加入的對照方法。
- Dunn et al. 2011 (Nature): 結構特徵 vs 詞彙資料的系統發生；語言演化的重要對照視角。
- Li 2008 (Formosan languages): 台灣 Formosan 語言多樣性；"recover Formosan" 結果的評判標準。
- Ross 2002 (Western Oceanic): Austronesian 子群研究的代表作；樹結構比較的參照。
- Wichmann et al. 2011 (ASJP v13): ASJP 資料庫本身的引用。
